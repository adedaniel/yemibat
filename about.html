About Us

YemiBat Building and Civil Construction Company is a leader in the construction of commercial, industrial and residential real estate.

Since the foundation of the company, the turnover of the company has doubled steadily every year, the number of employees proportionately grows. Today, it has more than 4,000 professionals. The company operates in Russia, CIS countries and Europe.
